FAQ

Common Questions

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?
Most don't realize it. Our AI sounds natural and conversational — like someone at the marina. Listen to the demo above and judge for yourself.
What if someone wants to talk to me directly?
The AI transfers them to your phone instantly. If you're available, great. If not, it handles the booking and notifies you.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We handle everything. You just show up for the one-hour onboarding call and tell us about your operation. You don't touch any technology.
Can I just use some of the modules?
Absolutely. Start with the core Website + Content package and add modules as you need them. Only want booking software during peak season? No problem. Add it, drop it, your call.
How is this different from an answering service?
An answering service takes a message. Our AI actually books the trip — checks your availability, confirms the date, collects customer info, and sends a confirmation. The booking is done before you even know someone called.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Plus we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee — if you're not happy, full refund. But most captains stay, because one booked trip pays for the whole month.
