Our Story

We Didn't Start in Tech.
We Started on the Water.

Fishbookers was born out of a simple frustration: we were losing bookings because we couldn't answer the phone.

You know the drill. You're 10 miles offshore with a customer fighting a lingcod. Your phone rings. By the time you get back to the dock, that caller booked with someone else.

We built an AI voice agent for our own charter operation — Pacific Charter Services in Charleston, Oregon. It worked. Calls got answered. Trips got booked. Confirmations went out. All while we were on the water.

Now we're making it available to every captain and guide who's tired of losing money to missed calls.

FAQ

Common Questions

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?
Most don't realize it. Our AI sounds natural and conversational — like someone at the marina. Listen to the demo above and judge for yourself.
What if someone wants to talk to me directly?
The AI transfers them to your phone instantly. If you're available, great. If not, it handles the booking and notifies you.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. If you can answer a 20-minute phone call about your charter, we can build everything. You don't touch any technology.
What booking systems do you work with?
We integrate with FareHarbor, Checkfront, Peek, and most major booking platforms. If you use something else, we'll make it work.
How is this different from an answering service?
An answering service takes a message. Our AI actually books the trip — checks your availability, confirms the date, collects customer info, and sends a confirmation. The booking is done before you even know someone called.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. But most captains stay — because one booked trip pays for the whole month.
